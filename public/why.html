Why Curious Trio?

Curious Trio is a project that's come out of hello dang's incubator. Dang Nguyen has spent fifteen years as an operating executive across digital businesses. Witnessing the Enshittification of marketplaces first-hand, one of the first challenges he wanted to address was the underlying pay-to-play dynamic that exists in product discovery.

We believe in enhancing your search experience by eliminating distractions. At Curious Trio, you wonâ€™t find any ads, just clean, unbiased search results to help you make informed decisions. We're working to make Curious Trio, product first, and retailer agnostic.

Our mission is to provide a space where consumers can research products and topics without being bombarded by paid advertisements, maximizing the relevance and neutrality of the information presented.

By focusing solely on delivering high-quality content without ads, we empower our users to research effectively, leading to better, more informed decisions without any commercial influence.

Transparently, the idea is to earn affiliate commisions on any purchases that are made from the site. To remove bias from this - we do not change search results based on product or retailer agreements. They're purely unfiltered (except for paid ads) results.
